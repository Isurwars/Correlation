Detailed Description

Computes all unique 3-body bond angles in the simulation cell.

This calculator iterates over every central atom and finds all unique combinations of its neighbors. It calculates the bond angle formed by the triad (Neighbor1 - Central_Atom - Neighbor2) taking into account periodic boundary conditions.

The computation is accelerated using Intel TBB (tbb::parallel_for) with thread-local storage (tbb::enumerable_thread_specific) to avoid locks when writing to the output tensor.
