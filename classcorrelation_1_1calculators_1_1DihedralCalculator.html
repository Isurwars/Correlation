Detailed Description

Computes all unique 4-body dihedral (torsion) angles in the simulation cell.

This calculator identifies all bonded quadruplets (A-B-C-D) and calculates the dihedral angle defined by the two intersecting planes: plane(A,B,C) and plane(B,C,D). The computation is accelerated using Intel TBB (tbb::parallel_for) with thread-local storage.
