Detailed Description

Computes and stores pairwise distances and bond angles in tensors indexed by element type.

This class efficiently finds all atom pairs within a cutoff radius and all unique bond angles, storing the results in multi-dimensional vectors (tensors) suitable for calculating partial distribution functions. The distance and angle calculation loops are parallelized with OpenMP.
